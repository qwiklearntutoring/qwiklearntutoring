Your Privacy Matters

Privacy Policy

We take your privacy seriously. Here's how we handle your information.

Last Updated: June 21, 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you use Qwiklearn Tutoring services, we may collect the following information:

  • Contact Information: Your name, email address, phone number, and any other details you provide when booking appointments or contacting us.
  • Student Information: Your child's name, grade level, and academic needs to help us provide tailored tutoring sessions.
  • Communication Data: Any messages you send us via email, our contact form, or other channels.
  • Technical Data: Basic information about your device and browser to help us improve our website experience.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Schedule and manage tutoring appointments
  • Communicate with you about sessions, scheduling, and updates
  • Provide the services you've requested
  • Improve our tutoring programs and website experience
  • Respond to your inquiries and support requests

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers: We use third-party services (such as Formspree for form submissions and email communications) that process data on our behalf. These providers are contractually obligated to protect your information.
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law, we may disclose information to comply with legal processes or protect our rights.

4. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request corrections to inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information (subject to legal obligations)
  • Withdraw consent for marketing communications at any time

To exercise these rights, please contact us at qwiklearntutoring@gmail.com.

6. Cookies and Tracking

Our website uses minimal cookies to improve functionality. You can adjust your browser settings to refuse cookies if you prefer.

7. Children's Privacy

We collect information about children only with parental consent, as part of our tutoring services. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children without verifiable parental consent.

8. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party sites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites and encourage you to review their privacy policies.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy policy, please contact us at:

Email: qwiklearntutoring@gmail.com
Website: qwiklearntutoring.com
