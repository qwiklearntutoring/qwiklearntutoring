Hi there, my name is Ms. Steiner.

I started tutoring while I was studying for my degree in University. In doing so I discovered a love and passion for teaching. I take great pride in guiding my students on the path to success. I have found that my excitement and love for the topics I teach can be infectious. It is a great joy to be able to share and spread my love for learning. I have taught students from preschool age up to and including college level. When I teach, I choose to create a well-rounded experience for my students where I target test taking skills, gaps in knowledge and teaching them to work independently. In my free time I love to hike, travel, watch physics documentaries and ponder my place in the universe.
